About Me

A short introduction and purpose of this portfolio.

Who I Am

Hello! I'm William Zhang, a high school student passionate about geography, spatial analysis, and data-driven storytelling. This portfolio showcases a variety of GIS projects I've created to explore real-world challenges using maps and data.

My Mission

My goal is to use GIS to inform policy decisions, identify social and environmental disparities, and make geographic data accessible to everyone. I believe in the power of maps to tell impactful stories and drive meaningful change.

Skills & Tools

  • ArcGIS Online, QGIS, and Leaflet.js
  • Python for data analysis and automation
  • Public datasets (Census, USGS, OpenStreetMap)
  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript for web mapping
